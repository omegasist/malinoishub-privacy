Privacy Policy – MalinoisHub

MalinoisHub is a community application for dog lovers.

Information Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share personal user data directly.

WebView Content

The app may display external web content using WebView. Any data collected by external websites is subject to their own privacy policies.

Cookies

The app itself does not use cookies.

Third-party Services

The app does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Children’s Information

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13.

Child Safety Standards

MalinoisHUB does not allow content that exploits or endangers children. Any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) is strictly prohibited.

Users can report inappropriate content through the app or by contacting the developer. Reported content is reviewed and removed immediately when it violates these standards.

MalinoisHUB cooperates with relevant authorities when required by law.
